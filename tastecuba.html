The key to the Gulf of Mexico

Cuba is an island in the Caribbean whose largest city is Havana, a city with 2 million inhabitants. Cuba is known globally for its manufacture of Cuban cigars, especially those of Havana, the Habanos. Its beautiful landscapes and beaches are magnificent. Cuban rum is one of the best in the world, the Havana Club Ron.The island is located north of the United States and the Bahamas, west of Mexico. The island is considered by many to be the key to the Gulf of Mexico due to its geographical location with respect to the Gulf.
