Welcome to Ressort Taste Cuba

Cuba, the key to the Gulf of Mexico, is an island in the Caribbean whose largest city is Havana, a city with 2 million inhabitants. Cuba is known globally for its manufacture of Cuban cigars, especially those from Havana, the Habanos. Its beautiful landscapes and beaches are magnificent. Cuban rum is one of the best in the world, the Havana Club Ron.The island is located north of the United States and the Bahamas, west of Mexico. The island is considered by many to be the key to the Gulf of Mexico due to its geographical location with respect to the Gulf. Our facility is located in the heart of the island, offering a wide number of amenities and activities that allow you to be in connection with local customs.
